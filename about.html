About Classic Muscle Car Ventures

Founded in the heart of the American muscle car era, Classic Muscle Car Ventures has been a beacon of automotive passion and craftsmanship for over four decades. Our journey began in the small town of Greensboro, where our founder, Hasan Hashim, turned his lifelong obsession with muscle cars into a thriving restoration business.

With a team of dedicated artisans and mechanics, we breathe new life into classic gems, from the raw power of the Ford Mustang to the iconic charm of the Dodge Challenger. Our restorations are more than just car repairs; they are a tribute to the golden age of American automotive excellence.

At Classic Muscle Car Ventures, we believe that every classic car has a story to tell. Our mission is to preserve these stories and pass them on to future generations, ensuring that the legacy of muscle cars continues to roar on the roads.
