Classic Car Technician

As a Classic Car Technician, you'll work on restoring the beauty and performance of vintage automobiles. We require a strong background in automotive repair and a passion for classic cars.

Responsibilities:

  • Performing maintenance and restoration tasks on classic muscle cars.
  • Diagnosing and repairing mechanical issues.
  • Customizing vehicles according to client specifications.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience as an automotive technician.
  • Strong understanding of automotive mechanics and maintenance.
  • Passion for classic muscle cars.
